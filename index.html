KARASU

If I were trapped in one city and had to eat one nation's cuisine for the rest of my life,
I would not mind eating Japanese.




Karasu is a Japanese restaurant & cocktail bar in Fort Greene.
The restaurant is a seasonal izakaya in spirit.
All dishes are meant for sharing and are informed by the idea of washoku,
a culinary philosophy that aims for balance across a meal
by covering a diversity of flavors, textures and colors among a spread of dishes.
Cocktails are reinterpreted classics, served alongside sake, shochu, and Japanese whisky
- all chosen to compliment the cuisine.
